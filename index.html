Mentor It is the best way to connect with mentors, you only need to search for your topic of interest, prefered rating, learning styles, choose a date, hour and place and finally pay your mentor. Then you are ready for an opportunity to learn something new!

You can be either a mentor or a mentee. To be a mentor you only need to post a mentoring opportunity and your grades, diplomas or any other document that certifies you are proficient in a topic. Just like that you are able to help other students and even earn a little extra money for it!

How it works

Every user can be a Mentor or Mentee. Mentors are students who teach or give advice on certain topics and Mentees are the students who attend this sessions.

Joining Mentees Mentors

To Join you only need to register and then access your dashboard that we refer to as MyDashboard. Then you are able to search for mentoring opportunities or post a mentoring

After login in or registering you are able to access your Dashboard in the top bar of the main menu by clicking on My Profile.

Then in you Dashboard you are able to search for an specific mentor depending on your selected preferences that consist on: topic, date, hour, costs, rating, school level and learning style. We take care of the rest.

We will create matches with different mentors and you can choose the one that best suits your needs. Or you can calso browse all the mentors registered in our site.

Finally you can register for a session and wait until the date and hour arrive to learn something new!

Be the difference! You can register a mentoring session where you take the role of being the one who helps other students understand or give advice on a certain topic.

To do this you simply need to click on Post Mentoring, then fill our the required information such as: topic, available hours, dates, semester, cost and a document such as grades or diploma that certifies you are proficient on a certain topic.

Finally you can post the mentoring and wait till a mentee sends you a request to receive a session.

Get ready to help others and even receive a monetary reward for it!
